About Word & Character Counter

Our Word & Character Counter is a comprehensive text analysis tool that provides detailed statistics about your text:

  • Word Count: Total number of words in your text
  • Character Count: Total number of characters including spaces
  • Characters (no spaces): Number of characters excluding spaces
  • Sentence Count: Number of sentences in your text
  • Paragraph Count: Number of paragraphs in your text
  • Reading Time: Estimated time to read the text (based on average reading speed)

Use Cases

  • Checking essay or article length requirements
  • Monitoring social media post character limits
  • Analyzing text content for SEO purposes
  • Estimating reading time for content
